# Methodology

The development of CyberRakshak was carried out using a systematic approach to ensure the successful design, development, and implementation of a digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness platform. The methodology consists of the following phases:

## 1. Requirement Analysis

* Identified major cybersecurity challenges faced by internet users.
* Studied common cyber threats such as phishing attacks, OTP frauds, UPI scams, identity theft, and social engineering attacks.
* Analyzed user requirements and project objectives.
* Defined the functional and non-functional requirements of the system.

## 2. Research and Literature Study

* Conducted a detailed review of existing cybersecurity awareness platforms and digital literacy programs.
* Examined government initiatives and awareness campaigns related to cybersecurity.
* Identified strengths and limitations of existing systems.
* Determined research gaps and opportunities for improvement.

## 3. System Design

* Designed the overall architecture of CyberRakshak.
* Planned the structure of educational modules and awareness resources.
* Designed user interaction workflows and navigation flow.
* Created the database structure and communication model between system components.

## 4. Frontend Development

* Developed the user interface using React.
* Designed responsive and user-friendly web pages.
* Ensured accessibility and ease of navigation for users.
* Organized educational content in an interactive format.

## 5. Backend Development

* Developed backend services using Spring Boot.
* Implemented application logic and request handling mechanisms.
* Established communication between frontend, database, and chatbot services.
* Ensured efficient processing of user requests.

## 6. Database Integration

* Integrated a database for storing system information.
* Managed educational content and user-related data.
* Implemented data storage and retrieval mechanisms.
* Ensured proper organization and maintenance of records.

## 7. AI Chatbot Integration

* Integrated an AI-powered chatbot into the platform.
* Enabled the chatbot to answer cybersecurity-related questions.
* Provided guidance regarding cyber threats and online safety practices.
* Improved user engagement through real-time assistance.

## 8. Testing and Validation

* Performed functional testing to verify system features.
* Conducted usability testing to evaluate user experience.
* Tested system reliability and performance.
* Identified and resolved issues discovered during testing.

## 9. Deployment and Future Enhancement

* Prepared the platform for deployment and evaluation.
* Collected user feedback to assess effectiveness.
* Identified potential improvements for future versions.
* Planned enhancements such as advanced learning modules, expanded multilingual support, and improved AI capabilities.

## Conclusion

The above methodology provides a structured framework for the development of CyberRakshak. By combining cybersecurity awareness, digital literacy resources, and AI-powered assistance, the platform aims to educate users, promote safe online practices, and contribute to a more secure digital environment.
