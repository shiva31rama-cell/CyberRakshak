# Abstract

The increasing dependence on digital technologies has significantly transformed communication, education, banking, commerce, and access to information. While these advancements have improved convenience and connectivity, they have also led to a rapid increase in cyber threats such as phishing attacks, OTP frauds, UPI scams, identity theft, social engineering attacks, fake websites, and online misinformation. A large number of internet users, especially students, senior citizens, and individuals with limited digital literacy, often lack the awareness and knowledge required to identify and respond effectively to these cyber threats.

CyberRakshak is a digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness platform developed to address this growing challenge by educating users about safe online practices and common cyber risks. The platform aims to provide reliable cybersecurity awareness through educational resources, interactive learning materials, and intelligent assistance. By combining cybersecurity education with modern technology, the system seeks to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible and understandable to a wider audience.

One of the key features of CyberRakshak is the integration of an AI-powered chatbot that assists users by answering cybersecurity-related queries and providing guidance regarding common online threats. The platform is also designed to support multilingual accessibility, allowing users from different linguistic backgrounds to access cybersecurity information more effectively. Through awareness modules and user-friendly learning resources, CyberRakshak encourages responsible digital behavior and promotes safe internet usage.

The primary objective of this project is to bridge the gap between cybersecurity knowledge and practical awareness among internet users. By helping individuals recognize cyber threats and understand preventive measures, the platform contributes to improving digital literacy and strengthening cyber resilience. CyberRakshak demonstrates how technology-driven awareness initiatives can support safer online experiences and contribute to the development of a more informed and cyber-aware society.
