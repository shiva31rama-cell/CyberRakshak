# Abstract

The rapid growth of digital technology has transformed the way people communicate, learn, conduct financial transactions, and access information. With the increasing use of the internet and digital services, cyber threats such as phishing attacks, OTP frauds, UPI scams, identity theft, fake websites, and social engineering attacks have become more common. Many internet users lack adequate cybersecurity awareness and are often unable to identify or respond effectively to such threats. As a result, there is a growing need for accessible and reliable platforms that can educate users about online safety and promote responsible digital behavior.

CyberRakshak is a digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness platform developed to address this challenge. The platform aims to improve cybersecurity awareness by providing users with educational resources, awareness materials, and guidance on safe online practices. It is designed to help individuals understand common cyber threats, recognize suspicious online activities, and learn preventive measures to protect their personal information and digital assets.

A key feature of CyberRakshak is its AI-powered chatbot, which assists users by answering cybersecurity-related queries and providing relevant information about online safety. The chatbot enhances user engagement by offering instant support and making cybersecurity knowledge more accessible. The platform also focuses on user-friendly design and accessibility, enabling individuals from diverse educational and linguistic backgrounds to benefit from its resources.

The development of CyberRakshak is based on the objective of bridging the gap between cybersecurity knowledge and practical awareness. By combining digital literacy education with intelligent assistance, the platform seeks to create a learning environment that encourages users to adopt safer online practices. The system not only raises awareness about cyber threats but also empowers users to make informed decisions while using digital technologies.

Through education, awareness, and interactive support, CyberRakshak contributes to the promotion of a safer digital environment. The project demonstrates how technology-driven awareness initiatives can play an important role in enhancing cybersecurity knowledge, strengthening digital literacy, and building a more informed and cyber-aware society.
